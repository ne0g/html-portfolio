Movies To Keep You Gripped by Neil

Here's a list of my top 3 movies

Predator

A fantastic Sci-Fi movie starring Arnold Schwarznegger in which an alien crash lands on the planet, hunting humans in the jungle.

Saving Private Ryan

A gritty world war 2 drama starring Tom Hanks and many more familiar faces. It follows a squadron of soldiers on their mission to save Private Ryan from behind enemy lines. Very dramatic, action packed, and a testament to the era.

Recommended to watch this with a surround sound speaker system, as this will really bring out punchy sounds.

Hereditary

A slow burn horror that involves a family, a tragedy, and cults that deal in generational curses. This movie is hands down one of my favourite modern horrors, with moments that will have you questioning your own perceptions!

Definitely recommended to watch this one once the kids are asleep!


Honorable Mentions

Mist

After a government experiment gone wrong, a portal to a hellish realm has opened up in a sleepy town located in the mountains of the US of A!

Also another horror, so consider watching with the lights off!
